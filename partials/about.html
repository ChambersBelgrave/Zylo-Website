About Lakay Events

Founded in 2018, Lakay Events was born from a passion for Caribbean culture and a desire to create unforgettable experiences that bring people together.

Our name "Lakay" means "home" in Haitian Creole, reflecting our mission to create a welcoming space where everyone can feel at home while enjoying the vibrant sounds and rhythms of the Caribbean.

We specialize in high-energy Soca events and laid-back Reggae nights, always with an emphasis on community, inclusivity, and positive vibes.
