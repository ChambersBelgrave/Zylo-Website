Frequently Asked Questions

All Lakay Events are strictly 21+ unless otherwise specified. Valid ID is required for entry.

All ticket sales are final. We do not offer refunds except in the case of event cancellation. Tickets may be transferred to another person free of charge.

VIP tickets include: reserved seating, priority entry, bottle service, and dedicated waitstaff. VIP areas have the best views of the stage.

We encourage festive, colorful attire that celebrates Caribbean culture. However, we only enforce a "no athletic wear" policy (no sweatpants, gym shorts, or sports jerseys).

Simply forward your ticket email to the new attendee. The name on the ticket does not need to match the attendee's ID.
